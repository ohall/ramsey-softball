This directory will contain sound effects for the game:

- pitch.mp3: Sound of the ball being pitched
- swing.mp3: Sound of the bat swinging
- hit.mp3: Sound of the bat hitting the ball
- home_run.mp3: Special sound for home runs
- foul.mp3: Sound for foul balls
- crowd.mp3: Ambient crowd noise
- cheer.mp3: Crowd cheering
- strike.mp3: Umpire calling a strike
- miss.mp3: Sound of a missed swing

Note: In a complete implementation, these sound files would need to be added here.
